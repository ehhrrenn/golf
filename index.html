🌬️ WIND MANAGEMENT

INTO THE WIND (Headwind) ▼
"When it's breezy, swing easy." Do not swing harder. Club up (e.g., 6-iron for 7-iron). Ball 1 inch back in stance. Smooth, 3/4 swing for piercing flight.
WITH THE WIND (Tailwind) ▼
Wind kills spin. Ball flies further but won't "bite" on greens. Trust carry but expect 5-10yd extra roll. Use 7W or Hybrid for steep descent.

🎨 SHOT SHAPING

CONTROLLED DRAW (R-to-L) ▼
Setup: Aim feet right, face at target. Swing: Inside-to-out along feet line. Feel right forearm rotate over left through impact. Great for distance.
CONTROLLED FADE (L-to-R) ▼
Setup: Aim feet left, face at target. Swing: Outside-to-in along feet line. "Hold off" the release; keep lead wrist firm. Great for soft green landings.

🏆 TOURNAMENT RULES

1. Middle of Green ▼
Ignore tucked pins. Aim for center of every green. A 2-putt par beats a "short-sided" double bogey every time. Protect the card.
2. Play for 2nd Shot ▼
If fairway is narrow or hazards are in play, take the 5-wood. A ball in the fairway is 100x better than 20 extra yards in the rough.
3. Putt Whenever Possible ▼
If path is clear, use the putter. Minimize the risk of a chunked specialty wedge under tournament pressure.
Keep your tempo smooth. Focus on the next shot.
